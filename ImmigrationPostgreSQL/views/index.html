About Bhutan Immigration

The Department of Immigration under the Royal Government of Bhutan is responsible for managing the entry and exit of foreign nationals to and from the Kingdom of Bhutan. We ensure secure, efficient, and visitor-friendly immigration services while maintaining the sovereignty and security of our nation.

Office Hours

Important Notice

All visitors to Bhutan must obtain proper visa and entry permits before arrival. Processing times may vary depending on the type of application and current volumes. Please apply well in advance of your intended travel date.

Security & Privacy

Your personal information is protected with state-of-the-art security measures. All data is encrypted and handled in accordance with Bhutan's data protection regulations and international standards.
